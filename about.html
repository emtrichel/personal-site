About Me

Hello! I’m Eric, and I’ve had quite a journey so far. Let me share a bit about myself:

Military Service

At the age of 18, fresh out of high school, I made a pivotal decision: to join the Marine Corps. Over the next 8 years, I dedicated myself to service, rising through the ranks to become a Sergeant. As a squad leader in multiple infantry units, I learned the true meaning of teamwork, discipline, and unwavering commitment.

Transitioning to Civilian Life

When my military service concluded, I found myself in the civilian world. Construction became my new path, but life had other plans. After losing my job, I faced a crossroads. It was then that I realized my true passion lay elsewhere—within the realm of computers.

Embracing Coding

One morning, fueled by determination, I decided to embark on a new adventure. Armed with curiosity and a thirst for knowledge, I began teaching myself how to code. The world of programming opened up before me, and I eagerly absorbed every concept, language, and algorithm.

Crafting Websites from Scratch

Currently, I’m knee-deep in my second “from scratch” website. It’s not just lines of code; it’s my canvas—a place where creativity meets functionality. On this site, you’ll find a collection of my work, a testament to my evolving skills and dedication.

Curious Bytes: A Blog

Curious Bytes is where I share my journey of self-teaching, technology, space exploration, scientific discoveries, and anything else that piques my interest. Whether it’s unraveling the mysteries of the cosmos or exploring the intricacies of a new programming language, I document it all.

Topics You’ll Find on Curious Bytes:

  • Self-Teaching Adventures: Join me as I navigate the vast landscape of programming, learning from tutorials, experimenting with projects, and celebrating breakthroughs.
  • Tech Insights: From web development to machine learning, I delve into the latest tech trends and share insights that inspire fellow learners.
  • Stargazing and Beyond: Space fascinates me—the celestial ballet of planets, stars, and galaxies. Expect posts about cosmic wonders and astronomical discoveries.
  • Science Unraveled: Curious about quantum physics, biology, or the mysteries of black holes? So am I! Let’s explore together.
  • Everyday Musings: Sometimes, it’s the little things—the cup of coffee, a sunrise, or a random thought—that spark inspiration. I’ll share those too.

Long-Term Aspirations

My ultimate goal? To become a software engineer. I envision myself contributing to innovative projects, solving complex problems, and shaping the digital landscape. The thrill of creating elegant solutions drives me forward.

Short-Term Focus

But I’m not waiting for the distant future. My short-term focus is crystal clear: I want to build websites for others. Collaborating with clients who have a vision, helping them bring it to life, and learning alongside them—that’s where my heart lies.

So, whether you’re a fellow learner, a visionary entrepreneur, or someone seeking a web presence, let’s connect. Together, we’ll turn ideas into reality, one line of code at a time. Thank you for joining me on this exciting journey!
